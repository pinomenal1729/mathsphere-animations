KEY INSIGHT
KEY INSIGHT

A linear map on ℝ² is completely determined by the images of e₁ and e₂. These two choices fix the entire transformation — every other vector's image follows from linearity: T(c₁e₁ + c₂e₂) = c₁T(e₁) + c₂T(e₂).

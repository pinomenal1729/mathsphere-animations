COMPOSITION = MATRIX MULTIPLICATION
KEY INSIGHT
KEY INSIGHT

Composition of linear maps corresponds to matrix multiplication: the matrix of S∘T is the product BA (S's matrix times T's matrix). Order matters: BA ≠ AB in general. This is why the order of layers in a neural network matters!
